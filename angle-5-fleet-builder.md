# Angle 5: The Fleet Builder (Transformation + Social Proof)

**Core Angle:** We've helped operators go from 3 trucks to 12. From one county to three. From surviving to building something worth passing down.

**Psychology:** Aspirational but grounded. Targets the identity shift from "guy with trucks" to "fleet owner building a legacy." This is about becoming something bigger.

**Target Customer:** Established towing company owner running 3-15 trucks who's turning down calls because they don't have enough trucks on the road. They're losing revenue every day they don't have another truck.

**Tone:** Aspirational but earned. Not hype. "We've seen this transformation happen hundreds of times, and here's how it starts."
