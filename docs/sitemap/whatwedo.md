# Tow Truck Financing Specialists  

**Tow Loans** (towloans.com — launching soon) focuses exclusively on financing tow trucks for established towing companies. This isn’t general equipment lending — towing is our niche, and our programs are built around how towing businesses actually operate and grow.

## Who We Work With  
We primarily serve established towing companies with **at least two years in business** that are looking to expand their fleet, replace aging equipment, or increase capacity.

## Equipment We Finance  

Our main focus is tow trucks, including:

- Wreckers  
- Rollbacks  
- Heavy wreckers  
- Rotators  
- Towing trailers (secondary focus)

## What Makes Our Financing Different  

### Zero-Down Tow Truck Financing  
Qualified businesses can add equipment without tying up working capital.

### Used Tow Truck Financing — No Age or Mileage Limits  
We’ve financed tow trucks **as old as model year 2000** with terms up to **54 months and zero down**. Approval is based on the business — not arbitrary vehicle age or mileage cutoffs.

### Private-Party Tow Truck Financing  
We regularly finance trucks purchased from:

- Individual sellers  
- Auctions  
- Online marketplaces (including Facebook Marketplace)  

If you find the right truck, we can usually finance it.

### Soft Credit Checks First  
Initial reviews are soft inquiries — no hard credit pull upfront.

### Deferred Payment Options  
Eligible customers can defer full payments for **up to 180 days**, with small **$99 touch payments** until the regular payment schedule begins.

### Choose-Your-Payment Transparency Tool  
Approved customers receive access to our proprietary tool that allows them to:

- Review real payment options  
- Adjust terms  
- Choose a payment structure that fits their cash flow  

### Fast Pre-Approval Without a Credit Pull  
Get pre-approved in about **30 seconds** and see an estimated payment range before submitting a full application.

### Towing-Specific ROI Calculators  
We provide profitability calculators tailored to towing operations that factor in expenses, utilization, and revenue potential — useful both for planning and decision-making.
