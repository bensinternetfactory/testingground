## Tow Truck Fleet Financing Program

Our financing program is designed for tow truck businesses that need to grow their fleet, even if they have not financed large truck purchases in the past.

### Qualification Tiers

You may qualify for up to **$350,000** if you have:

- A **640+ credit score**
- At least **2 years in business**
- At least **3 trucks in your fleet**

You may also qualify for up to **$350,000** with:

- A **640+ credit score**
- At least **7 years in business**
- **No minimum fleet size requirement**

You may qualify for up to **$500,000** if you have:

- A **640+ credit score**
- At least **5 years in business**
- At least **5 trucks in your fleet**

You may also qualify for up to **$500,000** with:

- A **640+ credit score**
- At least **10 years in business**
- **No minimum fleet size requirement**

This program is built for businesses that have traditionally paid cash for trucks and now want access to more capital.

Most lenders want to see comparable borrowing history before approving larger amounts, or they require a significant down payment. Our program is different. If you have an established business, we may consider higher financing amounts even if you have not borrowed that much before. Perfect for growing your fleet.

We will still review your payment history before final approval, but these guidelines help more operators qualify for the capital they need.
