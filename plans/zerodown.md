# /zero-down-tow-truck-financing
Keyword targeting = zero down tow truck financing

- We offer zero down on pretty much every approval we issue
- We can offer zero down on any truck age, regardless of milage

How do you qualify for zero down:
Path A (based on credit):
- Have at least a 640 credit score, 2 years time in business, and comparable debt
Path B (fleet operator)
- Have at least a 640 credit scote, 2 years time in business and 3 tow trucks in the fleet

Comparable debt for a commerical loan is when you have made at least 12 payments on an auto or installment loan where that loan was equal to at least 1/2 of the amount you are trying to borrow.
For example if you are trying to borrow $100,000. We want to see that you have at least made 12 payments on a loan worth 50,000
